Dr. Xiaofan Li

I'm currently an assistant professor at Nanjing University, China. I received my Ph.D. degree from Swinburne University of Technology and my Bachelor’s degree from Wuhan University. My research is primarily concentrated on cutting-edge domains within databases and LLMs, specifically focusing on: 1) Algorithm design for graph databases, particularly for the interdisciplinary frontier bridging LLMs and graph database systems. 2) Development and application of explainable GNNs. 3) Quantum database algorithms, aimed at exploring the potential for quantum computing to achieve significant acceleration of classical database problems.
